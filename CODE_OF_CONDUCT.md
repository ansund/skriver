# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `skriver` to be a welcoming and respectful project for contributors and users of all backgrounds.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful and constructive
- assuming good intent
- giving actionable feedback
- welcoming new contributors
- focusing on the shared goal of building a useful tool

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or threats
- bad-faith disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, discussions, commits, code, or other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Please report problems through the contact path in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) or [SUPPORT.md](./SUPPORT.md), depending on the issue type.
